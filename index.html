Climate Change

One of our biggest issues in society today is climate change. There is a lot of research to suggest that our climate is drastically changing for the worse. We will present many different reasons as to why the climate is changing. We will also present you the effects of the climate changing, backed by many other studies. We will also discuss what you, the average person, can do to help.

As you can see, the Earth's temperature has risen quite significantly in a span of only a little over 100 years. The different between the 1880s and the 2000s is roughly 1.5 degrees fahrenheit, which is quite significant for such a little amount of time. One of the most telling things is the increase between the 1910s, 1920s, and the 1930s as the Industrial Revolution began.
